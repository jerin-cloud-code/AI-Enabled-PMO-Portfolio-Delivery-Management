# Portfolio Governance Cadence & Pack Guide

## Governance Review Cadence
1. **Weekly Triage:** Front-door intake screening and initial duplicate check.
2. **Bi-Weekly Shaping Gate:** Business case elaboration and architectural alignment.
3. **Monthly Executive Portfolio Board:** Comprehensive RAG review, exception reporting, and formal decision approvals.

## Executive Governance Pack Structure (8 Slides)
Slide 1: Title & Metadata (Synthetic Disclaimer)
Slide 2: Executive Summary Narrative
Slide 3: Portfolio RAG & Health Matrix
Slide 4: Decisions Required (Ask / Options / Recommendation)
Slide 5: Key Risks & Issues Escalations
Slide 6: Dependencies & Inter-Initiative Blockers
Slide 7: Demand Pipeline & Shaping Funnel
Slide 8: Next Steps & Governance Actions
