# Comprehensive Research Summary — ai-enabled-portfolio-pmo

## Executive Overview
This research package establishes the primary principles and standards governing the design and implementation of `ai-enabled-portfolio-pmo`. The research encompasses four main pillars:

1. **Portfolio Governance & Intake Lifecycle:** Grounded in AXELOS MoP (`SRC-001`) and PMI Standards (`SRC-002`), establishing formal stage gates from Front-Door Intake through Shaping, Approval, and Active Monitoring.
2. **Jira Data Schema & Ingestion:** Mapped against Atlassian Jira CSV/JSON export specifications (`SRC-004`, `SRC-005`), supporting seamless import, normalization, and validation of enterprise portfolio records.
3. **Executive Reporting & Visualization:** Aligned with Gartner (`SRC-006`) and APM guidelines (`SRC-007`), defining standard one-page executive views and an 8-slide PowerPoint governance pack structure.
4. **AI Assurance & Trustworthy AI Controls:** Structured around NIST AI RMF (`SRC-008`), ISO/IEC 42001 (`SRC-009`), and EU Trustworthy AI principles (`SRC-010`), implementing a 6-part factual validation taxonomy (`VAL-01` through `VAL-06`), human-in-the-loop state transitions, and a deterministic offline fallback engine.
