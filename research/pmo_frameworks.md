# PMO Frameworks & Governance Best Practices Research

> **Source ID References:** `SRC-001` (AXELOS Management of Portfolios - MoP), `SRC-002` (PMI Standard for Portfolio Management 4th Ed), `SRC-003` (APM Body of Knowledge 7th Ed).

## 1. Portfolio Governance & Intake (Front-Door Process)

Standard portfolio management frameworks (MoP, PMI) partition portfolio governance into two main cycles: **Portfolio Definition** and **Portfolio Delivery**.

### Key Phases:
1. **Intake (Front-Door):** Initial proposal capturing problem statement, strategic alignment, sponsor, preliminary cost/benefit estimates.
2. **Shaping (Stage Gate):** Business case elaboration, architectural review, risk assessment, dependency mapping, and formal prioritisation.
3. **Approval:** Governance board (Investment Committee / Portfolio Board) approval decision: Approved, Deferred, Rejected, or Returned for Shaping.
4. **Active Delivery Monitoring:** Progress tracking via metrics, RAG health status, RAID log management, and variance tracking.

## 2. RAID & Decision Management Standards

- **Risks:** Potential events with Probability and Impact scoring. Must have named Risk Owner and Mitigation Plan.
- **Actions:** Discrete tasks assigned to individuals with explicit Target Completion Dates.
- **Issues:** Realised risks or active blockers requiring governance escalation.
- **Dependencies:** Inter-initiative linkages (Finish-to-Start, In-Flight Blockers, Shared Resource dependencies).
- **Decisions:** Formally recorded governance outcomes including Ask, Options Considered, Rationale, Approver, and Date.

## 3. Technology, Data, AI, Cyber Security & Fraud Portfolios

A balanced enterprise portfolio spans key functional domains:
- **Technology Delivery:** Infrastructure modernisation, legacy migration, API platform delivery.
- **Data & Analytics:** Data governance, pipeline automation, data mesh implementation.
- **AI & Machine Learning:** Model development, AI governance, automation pipelines.
- **Cyber Security:** Zero trust implementation, IAM modernisation, vulnerability management.
- **Fraud Prevention:** Real-time transaction monitoring, identity verification, anti-money laundering.
