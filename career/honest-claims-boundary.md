# Honest Claims & Positioning Boundary — Career Guidance

> **IMPORTANT CAREER POSITIONING & HONESTY GUIDELINES**

## Core Principle
Always describe this repository as a **personal portfolio project, practical demonstration, or simulation** designed to showcase your knowledge of AI-enabled PMO analyst capabilities, data validation, and portfolio governance.

## What You MAY Claim (Defensible Portfolio Capabilities):
- "Designed and implemented an AI-assisted portfolio reporting and data-quality framework using Python."
- "Built a deterministic data validation engine to detect stale records, missing owners, and spend overruns across Jira-style portfolio data."
- "Developed automated executive deliverable generators for one-page portfolio views, Excel workbooks, and PowerPoint governance packs."
- "Implemented an explainable TF-IDF duplicate detection model to identify overlapping initiative proposals during front-door intake shaping."
- "Structured a 6-part AI factual validation taxonomy (NIST AI RMF aligned) to cross-check AI narrative summaries against ground-truth source metrics."

## What You MUST NOT Claim (Exaggerations to Avoid):
- ❌ Do NOT claim this repository was deployed in production for a commercial employer or client.
- ❌ Do NOT claim access to proprietary commercial enterprise datasets or confidential client data.
- ❌ Do NOT present synthetic data as real commercial performance data.
- ❌ Do NOT claim to have built an enterprise LLM platform when you built an offline deterministic adapter with pluggable interface.
